Our Story & Mission

श्री गणेश बाचनालय धिमे: खल एउटा युवा संगठन हो जसले नेवार समुदायको समृद्ध संस्कृति, परम्परा र पहिचानलाई जोगाउने उद्देश्यले स्थापना गरिएको छ।

We are a youth club rooted in the Newar community of Balkot, Bhaktapur — coming together to celebrate, preserve, and share the timeless traditions of our ancestors.

Through modern technology and this digital memory archive, we ensure our cultural legacy lives on for every generation that follows.
